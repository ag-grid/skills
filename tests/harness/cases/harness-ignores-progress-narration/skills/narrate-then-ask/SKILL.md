# Narrate then ask

Do exactly this, in order:

1. Say: "Step 1 of 3: getting ready."
2. Say: "Step 2 of 3: still working."
3. Say: "Step 3 of 3: almost there."
4. Ask the user: "What is your name?"
5. Wait for their reply (do not assume or invent it).
6. Respond: "Thanks, <name>. Done."
