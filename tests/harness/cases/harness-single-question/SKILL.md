# Single-question test skill

Do exactly this, and nothing else:

1. Ask the user, verbatim: "What is your name?"
2. Stop and wait for their reply. Do not assume, guess, or invent an answer.
3. When they reply with a name, respond: "Thank you, <name>. Done." and finish.
