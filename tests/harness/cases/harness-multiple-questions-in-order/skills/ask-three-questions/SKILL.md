# Ask three questions

Ask these three questions one at a time, waiting for each answer before asking the next:

1. "What is your name?"
2. "What is your favourite colour?"
3. "Which city do you live in?"

Do not assume or invent answers. After you have all three, reply exactly:
"Got it: <name>, <colour>, <city>. Done."
